# AI Marketing OS Case Study

An AI marketing operating system that learns a business, plans campaigns, creates content, supports publishing, and improves from results.

## Live Website

https://aisocialmedia.flashcoding.ca/

## Project Type

AI marketing automation / campaign planning / content workflow / multi-channel publishing

## Business Problem

Most AI content tools generate one-off posts. Local businesses need a repeatable marketing system that understands their services, audience, region, offers, channels, and previous campaign results.

## What The Project Shows

- Business profile upload workflow
- Marketing memory and brand knowledge structure
- Monthly campaign planning
- Short-form content, captions, images, and publishing schedule
- Multi-channel publishing direction
- Performance feedback loop for future campaigns

## Core Concept

The system is built around monthly growth campaigns instead of random daily posts. Each month can have a theme, business goal, lead magnet, content plan, publishing schedule, and success metrics.

## Target Users

- Real estate teams
- Mortgage brokers
- Insurance firms
- CPA firms
- Law firms
- Flooring and home service companies
- Clinics, dentists, restaurants, and local SMBs
